Question -- 8 :

Chang had bought a new video game G.A.B - GRID AND BUGS . In this video game there would be a 8 x 8 grid having 64 cells in total. Now, the player has the option of filling any number of the cells with bugs . A single cell can contain a single bug. Now after this has been done , generation phase starts in which all the cells which are empty and have 2 or more neighbouring cells with bugs , get filled with a bug itself. After playing the video game several times , Chang noticed that sometimes after the generation phase all the cells have bugs . He derived that at least X cells should be filled with bugs or else the whole board cannot be filled after generation phase. Now you have to tell what X will be and why?
