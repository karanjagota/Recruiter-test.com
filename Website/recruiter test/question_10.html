Question -- 10 :

Harvard and Karen had bought a new video game for their PS4. It was a very interesting video game as it was a turn based game. There were several targets placed in a line. The player in each shoot down the targets , and after shooting the target it vanishes. Now , the player whose turn it is could shoot down at least 1 target and at most 2 targets where there should be no gap in between and no other target between those 2 targets . The person who shoots down the last target wins the unique game. Now , we are given that there are 2014 targets placed in a line. You have to tell who will win if karen takes the first turn and both of them play optimally ? And what will the answer in general case when we have N targets placed in a line ?
